O.R. Conspectus
Capital, Cogency & Capaciousness

Three operational ideals from the 2012 conspectus mapped to colour registers. Colour grammar is not decoration — it is a constraint system. As in linear programming, the feasible region is defined by the rules.

Billings Phenomenon · conspectus §1
As scarce as truth is, the supply has always been in excess of the demand. The colour system enacts the inverse: demand for clarity always exceeds supply of available registers. Scarcity is the grammar.
Capital
Control of means. The precondition for any operation. Without a defined resource set, throughput cannot be modelled.
Anchors: Regatta · Dragonfly · Cosmic
Register: Institutional · Legal · Deep
O.R. domain: Linear programming · Decision analysis
Pantone: 18-4039 · 19-4826 · 15-3909 TCX
Cogency
Analysis of measurement. The capacity to derive a conclusion from its instruments — not assert it from description.
Anchors: Citron · Rivulet · Scarlet
Register: Structure · Demonstrated · Blocking
O.R. domain: Regression · Pigeonhole · Ramsey
Pantone: 14-0827 · 14-4912 · 19-1558 TCX
Capaciousness
Strategy of operations. The system must hold more than it currently contains — negative space as designed feature, not absence.
Anchors: Cloud Dancer · Aqua Gray · Satin
Register: Ambient · Neutral · Surface
O.R. domain: Queueing theory · Simulation
Pantone: 11-4201 · 15-5205 · 20-0020 TCX/TPM
Diplomatic Missing Mass · conspectus §2
Gap between capability and institutional recognition. In colour terms: the tokens that exist but carry no semantic role assignment yet. Aqua Gray and Cloud Dancer occupy this zone in v2.0; their admission to v2.1 closes one such gap.

2027 Design Document
Proposed Directions

Six proposals drawn from the O.R. conspectus, the Pantone collation, and the systemic logic of the existing grammar. Each is a structural direction, not a cosmetic change.

P.01
The Domain Tier
Formalise a third tier of palette organisation: Domain (institutional, O.R.-anchored) alongside the existing Core and Portal tiers. Regatta becomes the primary domain anchor; a second domain colour — drawn from the 2027 Pantone forecast — fills the vacancy left by Nantucket's promotion to process-node.
Regatta primary Dragonfly secondary Cosmic tertiary
O.R. basis: Capital register formalised as tier. Maps to conspectus §1 — Control of Means. Decision science and linear programming anchored in a dedicated chromatic zone.
O.R.: Capital · Linear programming · Decision analysis
P.02
Light Mode Scaffold
Cloud Dancer (11-4201) and Aqua Gray (15-5205) together define a credible light-mode scaffold. 2027 should publish a parallel light-mode variable set — --surface becomes --cloud-dancer, --ink becomes --stretch-limo — with all semantic tokens retaining their hue identity but adjusted for contrast on a pale ground. Print and PDF artefacts would use the light scaffold by default.
Cloud Dancer ground Aqua Gray mid Mountain Trail rule
O.R. basis: Capaciousness — the system must hold more than it currently contains. A light scaffold is the designed negative space. Conspectus §3: interoperability requires the system to function across formats.
O.R.: Capaciousness · Simulation · Interoperability
P.03
Woodrose as Dim Narrative
Woodrose (16-1806) currently has a token but no formal semantic role. In 2027 it should be assigned explicitly: the dim register of Bordeaux, used where narrative content is cited or attributed rather than asserted. All blockquote, footnote, and legal-correlate text migrates from parchment-dim to woodrose. This creates a four-depth narrative stack: Bordeaux · Woodrose · Rose Brown · parchment-faint.
Bordeaux · assert Woodrose · cite Rose Brown · attribute
O.R. basis: Cogency — a claim is only as strong as its derivation stack. Colour encodes the depth of derivation. This is regression analysis applied to prose: the further from the primary source, the more visually receded.
O.R.: Cogency · Regression analysis · Derivation depth
P.04
Metallic Document Layer
The three TPM metallics — Graphite (20-0188), Satin Slipper (20-0020), Grape Frost (20-0070) — currently anchor print and physical artefact contexts implicitly. 2027 should formalise a Document Layer variable set: --D-substrate, --D-rule, --D-accent that maps these metallics to defined roles in print, PDF, and formal submission contexts. The Gold token's relationship to Amberlight (14-1217) and Dusky Citron (14-0827) should be explicitly resolved: two variants for screen vs. print.
--D-substrate · Graphite --D-rule · Satin --D-accent · Gold
O.R. basis: Material Effects (conspectus §1 — Pantex section). Throughput varies by medium. The grammar must specify constraints for each output format as a distinct feasible region.
O.R.: Material effects · Operational constraints · Feasible region
P.05
Emergency Logistics Palette
Conspectus Case Ten — Emergency Logistics for the Humanitarian Sector — identifies routing and allocation under uncertainty as a distinct operational mode. 2027 should define an Emergency Sub-palette: Rivulet (confirmed routing), Scarlet (critical path), Regatta (institutional brief), Aqua Gray (ambient capacity), Citron (decision node). These five define the chromatic feasible region for crisis-mode documents — EU humanitarian logistics briefings, emergency operations annexes.
Rivulet · confirmed Scarlet · critical Regatta · brief Aqua Gray · capacity
O.R. basis: Case Ten directly. Critical routing under uncertainty requires a palette that distinguishes confirmed from provisional from blocked with no ambiguity. The sub-palette is a restricted grammar — a subset of the full system with stricter semantic enforcement.
O.R.: Emergency logistics · Critical path · Allocation under uncertainty
P.06
The Pigeonhole Rule
From the conspectus: Pigeonhole Principle — if more items than containers, at least one container holds more than one. Applied to the colour grammar: the system currently has nineteen named tokens and eleven semantic roles, meaning eight tokens have no exclusive container. The 2027 grammar should enforce the pigeonhole constraint — every named token must either have a unique role or be retired. Woodrose, Mountain Trail, Aqua Gray, and Stretch Limo are the candidates for formal role assignment or explicit retirement notation. No new tokens should be admitted to v2027 unless a role vacancy exists first.
Woodrose → assign Mountain → assign Aqua Gray → assign Stretch Limo → retire?
O.R. basis: Pigeonhole Principle (conspectus §1). Existence of solutions requires that constraints be satisfiable. An overcrowded semantic container cannot close. The grammar must be a closed system — Ramsey Theory applies: sufficient structure guarantees a pattern will emerge, but only if the token set is bounded.
O.R.: Pigeonhole · Ramsey theory · Existence of solutions

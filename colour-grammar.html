Axiom bar · bordeaux register
Structural note · grammar and law converge
Local rules that cannot close the system from inside require an external constraint to close them.
The writing rules for this project follow the same logic as the legal case. Individual rules cannot close the system alone; K.3 only emerges when they compose under the right conditions.
§ 08
Task Items

Scarlet for blocking OIs, dragonfly for non-blocking. Orbitron numerics, IBM Plex Mono task names, EB Garamond body copy.

OI-01
Blocking
Write the incompetence argument properly
The 2009 and 2025 positions are mutually exclusive. The breach argument has not been written yet. It needs a dedicated passage built step by step from the O'Keeffe irrationality standard.
OI-02
Blocking
Resolve the Ombudsman complaint conflict
One added paragraph names the annual report mechanism as operative remedy. Currently the draft identifies the conflict as an irony. It needs to name it as a structural challenge.
OI-03
Not blocking
Show the three-court architecture from the law itself
Instead of saying "these bodies don't overlap," show text by text exactly why no single authority can substitute for any other. Not blocking, but should be completed in the same session.
